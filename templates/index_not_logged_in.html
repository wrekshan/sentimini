Emojinsei is a smart emotion tracking app

After you'll sign up, you'll recieve text messages asking about how you are experiencing emotions during that present moment.

Just breath and pay attention to yourself and the world. Respond with a number between 0 and 10. It is that simple.

Emojinsei might be good for you

You'll be reminded to breath and pay attention to your present moment, living in this wonderful world.

This is a easy structure, designed to require very little work, for you to learn about emotions.

It is designed to provides meaningful data about how you experience emotions.

Emojinsei might be good for society

Emojinsei is designed to provide meaningful data about how large groups of people are experiencing emotions. At minimum, this could look like a map of emotions across the cities, states, and countries.

Psychology research: What emotions do people experience? Are angier people happy? Is money related to happiness?

Providing data to make better civic decisions: Are people in cities happier than rural? What would the effect of installing public libraries be?
