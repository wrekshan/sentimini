Sentimini

Sends texts and collects the responses

You can have texts of whatever you want or you can sign up for predefined lists of texts. Click here to sign up for texts.

All replies to texts are collected and stored. Click here to see your data.

Individual

Getting texts can be great!

Get reminders to be mindful, that you love the people around you, or that can improve your life.

Load up a feed of your favorite quotations or inspirational sayings.

Remind yourself why you are trying to change a behavior.

Personal Informatics

This is a great system to collect data about human experience because the content of and times of the texts are random.

Sentimini can be used an emotion tracker, as a gratitude journal, etc. Use it to track your pain or how much you've exercised.

With others

Create programs for others

Use as a way for a therapist or doctor to communicate with a patient. Can introduce specific circulum or track specific symptoms.

Use to see how groups are doing

Sentimini could be used in research or corporate wellness. In addition to providing text based communications, sentimini can collect the responses.

Open Data Set

Sentimini will ideally be an open dataset.
