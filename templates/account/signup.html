The Process

Receive a text

For example:
How much happiness is in your present moment (0-10)?

Breathe

Take a second. You are alive in this wonderful world, feeling a multitude of different emotions.

Look for the emotion

You could be angry. You could be happy. You could be both. But look for the emotion in the experience. Is it there? What does it feel like?

Rate from 0-10

Don't worry. A number will never capture your experience, so you can't do this wrong.

Easy

All you have to do is sign up and respond to a few texts a day asking about a wide range of emotions.
You can take breaks whenever you need by texting, "pause".

Smart

Since the time and the emotion of the texts are randomized, quantitative patterns can be examined. What emotions exist? Do I experience emotions like my fellow humans?

Kind

Your time is respected by requiring little effort and providing a sensible structure to evaluate quantitative patterns of your emotional life and experience emotions in your present moment.
