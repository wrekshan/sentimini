Why

Texts can reach almost anyone, are inexpensive, and are very quick.

Texts are a great way to learn about human life.
  • Nobel wining economist, Daniel Kahneman, uses experience sampling
  • Texts have been used to map happiness at the London School of Economics
  • At least 153 clincial trials have utilized SMS in some way and 480 have evaluated mindfulness (from the NIH Clinical Trials Catalog accessed through enigma.io)
    • Texts can remind, inspire, or teach. They can enhance life in any number of ways.
  • Journaling about dreams, increases likelihood of dreams
  • Mindfulness has been shown to be helpful for a wide range of things
  • Reminders to be mindful could go a long way in increasing wellness
  • Explore all the different uses of sentimini.
    • Therefore, the aim of Sentimini is to provide inexpensive and quick tools learn about and enhance human life. This ideally will include open dataset and free statistically-informed tools to assist in the quantitative investigation of human experience.

    Sentimini is currently in beta. I (William Rekshan) developed this application because I wanted these tools to help me in my own life and I wanted to give back to this world. Over the last 10 years of working on research studies, I was able to travel the world, learn about neuroscience, statistics, and computer programming. I learned a lot of this through open science and data resources found on the internet. These experiences enhanced my life. I would like to give back.
