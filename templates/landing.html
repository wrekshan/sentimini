Easily obtain quantiative data about your life

Why?

After working in psychology and neuroscience research for nearly 10 years and meditating for nearly 5, I wanted to make something that would be beneficial in research and easy to use. So, I set out to create an emotion tracker, something to help me in a meditation class. Along the way I discovered three things:

1. Text messages are a scalable, accessible, low cost, and low time way to do research about the human experience. Nobel wining economist, Daniel Kahneman, uses experience sampling, texts have been used to map happiness at the london school of econmoics, Matt Killingsworth used texts to investiage happiness while at Harvard, 153 clincial trials have utilized SMS in some way and 480 have evaluated mindfuless (obtained by searching 'SMS' and 'mindfulness' in the NIH Clinical Trials Categlogue accessed through enigma.io.
----- People should have these tools to learn about their own lives.

2. Just receiving text messages could be beneficial. Journaling about dreams, increases likelihood of dreams. Thinking about pain and depression and allievate it. Reminders to be mindful could go a long way in increases wellness. Explore all the different uses of sentimini.
----- People should have these tools to learn about their own lives.

In other words, texts are a very cost-effective way of obtaining data about human experience, especially if the system is easy to use (let me know how I can do better!). I truly believe that data can improve our lives, allow for better decisions, and alleviate suffering. I think that being curious about our world and other human beings can lead to better interations and society. I also believe that transparency is crucial for efficient scientific progress.

Sentimini is currently in beta. The intention is to develop free tools and an open data set of human experience that contributes to understand and improving the human experience as measured by the number of people using this site and contributions to applied scientific projects.
