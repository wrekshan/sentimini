Beta

Sentimini is in beta status.

This is a minimum viable product but work still needs to be done to ensure a fulfilling and technological stable experience. As such, it is 'use at your own risk', especially when it comes to data security.

Next major steps:
  • Sets of text centered around a specific experience
  • Greater control of text timing
  • Sharing of data for use with professional helpers
  • Sharing of data in open science / open data frameworks

    Personal note

    I know that data can transform our lives and ways of being. I've worked in psychology and neuroscience research for nearly 10 years, 4 of these as a senior biostatician (check out my LinkedIn or scientific papers).

    I know that just asking questions and being curious the world can improve my life. I've learned this though meditating in a structured way for 5 years (and reading about mindfulness in the scientific literature).

    As simple as this is, Sentimini is the result this experience. Sentimini just seeks to provide reminders to pay attention to this world and your life, in a way that is most meaningful to you. You can add any content that you wish or choose from lists of texts. The whole goal is to make Sentimini as easy a possible and in a way that respects your rich experience of life.

    As a statistician, I wanted this experience to provide a structure to sensibily answer quantitative questions about this world. Randomization is crucial for this (in addition to removing the burden of having to remember to record something). Graphs and tables are provided for all texts and responses.

    I hope that Sentimini is helpful to you. If you have any thoughts, comments, or suggestions, please email me at XXXXX@blah.com

    Enjoy!
