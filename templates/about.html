Overview

Sentimini sends periodic text messages with varying content at semi-random times.

You can change the content and the timing at feeds.

It displays the responses in downloadable tables and figures, making it easy to obtain data about your experience.

You can view and access your data at the home page.

Beta

Sentimini is in beta status.

This is a minimum viable product but work still needs to be done to ensure a fulfilling and technological stable experience. As such, it is 'use at your own risk', especially when it comes to data security.

Next major steps:
  • Sets of text centered around a specific experience
  • Greater control of text timing
  • Sharing of data for use with professional helpers
  • Sharing of data in open science / open data frameworks
