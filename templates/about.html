Overview

Sentimini sends periodic text messages with varying content at semi-random times.

You can change the content and the timing at settings.

It displays the responses in downloadable tables and figures. You can view and access these at the home page.

You can learn more at this blog post.

Beta

Sentimini is in beta status.

This is a minimum viable product but work still needs to be done to ensure a fulfilling and technological stable experience. As such, it is 'use at your own risk', especially when it comes to data security.

Next major steps:
  • Sets of text centered around a specific experience
  • Greater control of text timing
  • Sharing of data for use with professional helpers
  • Sharing of data in open science / open data frameworks

    • Using the following use cases at examples:
  • Slow learning / Courses / Guided Experiences
  • Behavior Change Assist
  • Wellness assist - evaluation
  • Wellness assist - diagnsosis/communication

    • Learn more at this blog post.

    Open Data

    By default, you are signed up to participate in the Open Data Project.

    There are three aims:
  • Improving user experience
  • Ensuring validity of method (can one actually measure happiness or pain in this way?)
  • Understanding human experience

    • Learn more here.

    Donate

    A main aim of Sentimini is to be self-supported to establish a structure that:
  • Encourages development to make the best experience possible
  • Allows for an uncompromised ability to pursue open source / data / science

    • The plan is to charge a small amount (around 5 USD per month) for an advanced feature (probably the ability to schedule texts at specific times).

    Until that time, please consider donating!
