Overview

Sentimini sends periodic text messages with varying content at semi-random times. You can change the content and the timing at settings.

It displays the responses in downloadable tables and figures. You can view and access these at the home page.

You can learn more at this blog post.

Beta

Sentimini is in beta status. There is a minimum viable product but work still needs to be done to ensure a fulfilling and technological stable experience. As such, it is 'use at your own risk', especially when it comes to data security.

Next major steps:
  • Curated experiences - Sets of words centered around a specific experience (i.e. mindfulness, depression, quitting smoking, dream journal, etc)
  • Greater control of text timing
  • Sharing of data for use with healthcare providers/professional helpers
  • Sharing of data in open source / open science / open data frameworks

    • Using the following use cases at examples:
  • Slow learning / Courses / Guided Experiences
  • Behavior Change Assist
  • Wellness assist - evaluation
  • Welness assist - diagnsosis/communication
  • Learning about this world.

    • Learn more at this blog post.

    Open Data

    By default, you are signed up to participate in the Open Data Project, which has three goals:
  • Improving user experience
  • Ensuring validity of method (can one actually measure happiness or pain in this way?)
  • Understanding human experience

    • Research questions, plans, data and results are shared here.

    Submit questions and learn more here.

    Blog

    Any help files, research plans and results, development plans and advancements are posted on the blog.

    Please contact us if you have a question, need help, want to see a new feature, want to see a new analysis.

    Read more here.

    Donate

    A main aim of Sentimini is to be self-supported to establish a structure that 1) encourages development to make the best experience possible and 2) allow for an uncompromised ability to pursue open source / data /science.

    The plan is to charge a small amount (around 5 USD per month) for an advanced feature (probably the ability to schedule texts at specific times).

    Until that time, please consider donating!
