Text Commandsinfo_outline
stop
You can just text, "stop", to stop receiving all text messages.
start
If you have previously texted "stop", you can text "start", to begin receiving messages again.
new:
You can create a new text to be sent by texting, "new:", followed by the text that you want to receive. This will take your default timing. You can change your default timing here.
For example, if you want to receive the text, "Focus on your breath!", you can just text sentimini, "new: Focus on your breath!"
