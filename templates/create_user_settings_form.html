What will happen next?

  • You will recieve texts at random times once you complete this form.
  • They will ask about the emotions you are experiencing in your present moment.
  • For example:
    • "How much joy is in your present moment (0-10)?"
    • Breath, think, and look for joy in your world.
      • If there is no joy, reply with 0.
      • If there is only joy, reply with 10.
  • If you forget or miss a prompt, don't worry about it. Just respond to the prompt you have just recieved and ignore the ones you missed.

    • You have control!

  • You can edit the prompts you recieve (Edit Settings > My Prompts).
  • You can pause the prompts by:
    • Texting: "pause"
      • This will pause for 1 day
    • Texting: "pause 3 days" or "pause" with a duration of time
      • This will pause 3 days or whatever time you say
    • Using the pause links at the top of the screen
