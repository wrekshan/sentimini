The world is beautiful and good!

Sentimini can help you explore this world by sending you texts at periodic times throughout your day.
You can schedule whatever text you want, at whatever time you want. Sentimini will collect any replies you make and let you download them in simple spreadsheets. These are the ingredients for a light-weight, human-centric, tool for mindfulness, behavior change, and research/health care.
Sentimini is in beta, free to use, and signing up takes less than 2 minutes.
Create a text
This could be a reminder to have better posture, that you love your family, to make a haiku, experience emotions, or anything else.
Set the frequency
Do you want to receive the text every 3 hours? Every 3 days? Every weekday between 9am and noon? You have control.

Who made this? Why was this made?

Who made this?

It is sometimes strange to see a website made by an unknown person, so I wanted to tell you a bit about myself. My name is William Rekshan. I made Sentimini because I took a class on meditation and emotions, but I kept on forgetting to pay attention! I looked around for an emotion tracker that would help me and I found none that were quite right.
So I made Sentimini. I've personally used it for over a year. I've found it truly amazing to be so caught up in my throughts only to receive a text encouraging me to just breathe, or that I will die, or that I actually do love the people in my life.
I've worked in psychology research for nearly 10 years! Please see my LinkedIn or published research papers for an idea of who I am. I've learned so much about statistics, this world, and what it means to be human through this work. I just wanted to give back!
Please reach out if you have any questions or thoughts! I'd love to speak with you! I hope you enjoy!
Thanks!

Why was this made?

Sentimini was originally intended to be an emotion tracking app. When I looked for emotion tracking apps, I never found really solved my problem....that I just kept on forgetting to pay attention to my emotions. I really needed an app that would remind me at random times throughout the day.
I've worked in neuroscience and psychology research for almost 10 years, with 4 of those as a statistician, so I knew immediately that this is a wonderful, less biased way of quantitatively exploring lived human experience. In fact, there is already a lot of great work in this area: track your happiness, mappiness, and much of Daniel Kahneman's work. While working to develop fMRI based neurofeedback to assist people experiencing chronic pain, I witnessed people transform their lives by being curious about their experiences (in this case mindfullness while experience pain).
I wanted to be able to learn about others through research and offer tools to improve life that I would use myself. So, I made Sentimini for myself. I've used it to improve my own life. I know what I want to do in life. I know what I need to do to make myself happier. I just need a small nudge to remember. I hope that others will find it helpful. I hope to be able to do open-source citizen science with it.
Thanks!
