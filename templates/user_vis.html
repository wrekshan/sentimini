The Process

Receive a text

For example:
How much happiness is in your present moment (0-10)?

Breathe

Take a second. You are alive in this wonderful world, feeling a multitude of different emotions.

Look for the emotion

You could be angry. You could be happy. You could be both. But look for the emotion in the experience. Is it there? What does it feel like?

Rate from 0-10

Don't worry. A number will never capture your experience, so you can't do this wrong.
